What Is This

High-Stakes Military PvP.
Where Odds Are A Weapon.

A tactical military arena fused with a risk-and-reward system. Your gear, your boosts, your rewards — all tied to wagers. Win big, or watch everything fall into enemy hands.

🎲
01 — WAGER
Stake Your Resources
Before every deployment, choose how much to risk. Higher wagers unlock stronger gear — prototype weapons, Tier 4 armor, squad-wide buffs. But losing means everything you staked falls into enemy hands.
⚔️
02 — DEPLOY
Fight Across Dynamic Maps
Drop into war-torn combat zones with tactical chokepoints, destructible cover, and randomized events that shift the flow mid-match. Capture points, sabotage missions, supply drops — every objective flips the odds.
🎰
03 — GAMBLE
Chance Crates & Risk Modifiers
Mid-match chance crates offer damage buffs, armor upgrades, or credit jackpots. Activate risk modifiers — double your damage for 20 seconds, but take double damage too. Extraction stakes let you leave early or push for more.
🏆
04 — COLLECT
Multiply Rewards, Climb Ranks
Top performers spin the match-end roulette for rare gear, cosmetics, and massive credit boosts. Progress through prestige paths: High Roller, Tactician, Survivalist, or Gambler. The bold rise fastest — the reckless start over.

The World

A Post-Collapse Warzone
Where the Odds Are Unstable.

Military factions once fought over experimental probability technology. When the experiments failed, the region became a sprawling wasteland of malfunctioning tech, mutated threats, and abandoned casinos still running on corrupted systems.

🏙️
The Fallen City
Danger ★★★★★
Skyscrapers, collapsed bridges, flooded streets, and live casino districts still pumping neon. High density PvP. Credit Storms spawn here most frequently. The Neon Graveyard district offers the best loot — and the most danger.
🏜️
The Dead Expanse
Danger ★★★★★
A desert wasteland with buried bunkers and crashed military aircraft. Dust storms reduce visibility to near zero. Buried vault terminals can be activated for rare loot — but trigger nearby zombie hordes when you do.
🌲
The Quarantine Forest
Danger ★★★★★
Overgrown labs, mutated wildlife, and fog-covered paths where screamer variants roam. Contains the densest concentration of Hazard-Class gear — if you survive the Spitter hordes and navigate the quarantine locks.
🏭
The Iron Valley
Danger ★★★★★
Industrial factories, rail yards, and abandoned military depots. Ironvale Defense Systems' headquarters sits here — stripped, looted, and now occupied by rogue faction AI. Excellent for Tier 2-3 weapon farming.
🎰
The Neon Graveyard
Danger ★★★★★
A ruined entertainment district still crackling with broken signage. Slot machines still dispense random loot. Roulette tables trigger environmental modifiers. Boss AI runs the casino vaults — and it doesn't lose gracefully.
☢️
The Black Zone
Danger ★★★★★ ANOMALOUS
The deepest region. Physics distort, visibility warps, and elite Tier 5 enemies patrol without patterns. Anomaly-powered weapons only spawn here. Requires high-wager clearance to enter. Most players don't come back up.
